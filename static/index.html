Alpha i Alpha
Your excess return vs the S&P 500, calculated as TWR minus S&P return. Positive = you outperformed the market; negative = the index beat you.
TWR i Time-Weighted Return
Strips out the effect of when you deposited money. Best for comparing your investment decisions against a benchmark like the S&P 500. This is how fund managers are evaluated.
MWR i Money-Weighted Return
Reflects your actual experience as an investor — it accounts for when and how much you deposited. Higher if you put more money in before good periods, lower if you deposited before downturns.
